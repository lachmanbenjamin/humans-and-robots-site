Website tracking and advertising

What we use, and why.

Humans & Robots uses the Meta Pixel on our website to understand how visitors interact with our pages, measure the effectiveness of our marketing, and improve future advertising. The Meta Pixel may collect information such as pages visited, actions taken on the site, browser and device information, and other usage data.

Meta may use this information to provide measurement, analytics, and advertising services, including helping us understand whether someone who saw or clicked an ad later visited our website. Meta may also use this information according to its own privacy policies.

Your choices

Controlling how your data is used.

You can control how Meta uses data for advertising through your Facebook or Instagram ad preferences. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings — most browsers let you block or delete cookies on a per-site basis.

If you'd rather not be tracked by the Meta Pixel at all, blocking third-party cookies or using a tracker-blocking browser extension will prevent it from loading.

Other tools on this site

Beyond the Meta Pixel.

We also use a lightweight visitor analytics tool to understand traffic patterns at a high level (which pages get visited, where visitors came from). We do not sell visitor data, and we do not share it outside of the providers we use to operate the site and our marketing.
