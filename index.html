About

Built on 20 years in the trenches.

Humans and Robots was founded by a marketing technologist with two decades of experience in social media, community management, and digital strategy — working with nonprofits, startups, and growth-stage companies across fundraising, product launches, and community building.


We started this consultancy because we kept seeing the same pattern: companies know AI is important, but they don't know where to start. We bridge that gap — implementing AI tools that produce real output from day one, not slide decks about what AI could theoretically do.


Our clients have used our AI-augmented approach to produce full content calendars in hours instead of weeks, build financial models with 900+ live formulas, launch websites in a single session, and file regulatory documents that position them at the forefront of their industry.


We also work with trades and service businesses — contractors, signage companies, skilled trades — that have been running on paper invoices and filing cabinets for decades. If your operations still live in PDFs and spreadsheets, we turn that into a modern, automated workflow without disrupting what already works.
