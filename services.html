Service 01

Social Media

A content engine that produces real posts, reels, and stories every week — not just a strategy deck. We handle the strategy, creation, scheduling, and reporting end-to-end, with AI doing the heavy lifting and a human owning the voice.

What's Included

  • Content strategy & calendar. Monthly themes, campaign arcs, and a week-by-week posting calendar tied to your goals.
  • AI-powered creation. Copy, images, short-form video, and carousels — drafted with AI, edited by a human, approved by you.
  • Multi-platform scheduling. Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — scheduled and cross-posted natively.
  • Performance analytics. Monthly reporting on what's working, what isn't, and where to shift spend or effort.
  • Community engagement. Comment monitoring, DM triage protocols, and response templates your team can use.

Best Fit

Growing brands and nonprofits that need a consistent social presence but can't justify a full content team. Clients typically get 3–5x the output of a part-time social manager at roughly the same cost.

Recent example: ongoing content management for a peace-building nonprofit — carousel posts, video scripts, and shot lists — delivered in under 500 AI credits per post across multiple platforms.

Service 02

CRM & Marketing

A working CRM, real lead capture, and automated nurture sequences — set up, cleaned up, and running. We stand up HubSpot (or optimize what you already have) so every lead is tracked, every conversation is logged, and every pipeline stage has a next step.

What's Included

  • CRM setup & cleanup. HubSpot (or your preferred stack) configured with custom properties, pipelines, deal stages, and hygiene rules.
  • Lead capture & qualification. Forms, routing rules, and AI-assisted scoring so the right leads reach the right person fast.
  • Email sequences & nurture flows. Drafted with AI, tuned for your voice, and A/B tested — from cold outreach to post-sale follow-up.
  • Pipeline reporting. Dashboards your team will actually use — forecast, conversion, velocity, and win/loss.
  • Marketing automation. Workflows that connect your CRM, email, ads, and analytics into one closed loop.

Best Fit

Sales-led or marketing-led teams that have outgrown spreadsheets, inboxes, or a CRM that no one actually uses. Ideal for companies spending more than $5K/month on marketing with no clear attribution.

Recent example: a signage company with 8,000 PDFs and zero CRM went from filing cabinets to a live, segmented HubSpot database with automated nurture sequences in 48 hours.

Service 03

Workflow Implementation

Operations modernization for businesses still running on paper, PDFs, and tribal knowledge. We take the workflow that already works — estimates, invoices, dispatch, follow-up — and rebuild it as an automated system your team will actually adopt.

What's Included

  • Legacy document migration. Illustrator PDFs, scanned invoices, QuickBooks exports — extracted, structured, and imported into modern tools.
  • Document processing & data organization. OCR pipelines, normalization, deduplication, and a clean source of truth.
  • CRM for trades & service businesses. Built around how your crew actually works — not a generic SaaS template.
  • Automated scheduling, dispatch, and follow-up. Fewer dropped balls, fewer "who was supposed to call them?" moments.
  • Financial workflow automation. QuickBooks, Stripe, and accounting workflows that used to take hours now run in minutes.

Best Fit

Contractors, trades, distributors, and service businesses where the operations are solid but the systems are stuck in 2005. If your office manager is the single point of failure, we can help.

Recent example: a food distribution client's monthly QuickBooks reconciliation went from a 4-hour manual process to a 2-minute automated workflow — without changing the underlying accounting system.
