The origin

Why we started this.

We kept watching smart businesses stall at the same line: we know AI matters, but we don't know where to start. Leadership had read the posts. Teams had tried ChatGPT. Maybe they'd spent a weekend with Zapier. Then Monday came and nothing in the real workflow had changed.

H&R exists to close that gap. We tell you where AI pays off for your business specifically, how to wire it into the work your team already does, and how we'll measure the payoff — before we ship anything.

How we think

Human strategy. Robot leverage.

Every useful AI engagement we've ever run started with a human question — not a model. What outcome do we need? What's the hardest part of getting there? Where do the hours go? Only after those answers do we decide which robot to reach for.

We believe AI is a force multiplier for the people already doing the work — not a replacement. The best H&R clients end up with teams who are sharper, faster, and more ambitious. Robots free them to do the stuff that actually compounds.

What we've shipped

Across wildly different industries.

H&R clients have used our approach to produce full content calendars in hours instead of weeks, build financial models with 900+ live formulas, launch websites in a single session, create client-ready proposals and documents in minutes, file regulatory documents that position them at the forefront of their industry, and migrate 8,000 PDFs into a working CRM in 48 hours.

We also work with trades and service businesses — contractors, signage companies, skilled trades — still running on filing cabinets, spreadsheets, and PDFs. If your ops work but your systems are stuck in 2005, we help you modernize without breaking what already works.

How we bill

Outcome-priced. No lock-in.

Engagements typically start around ~$3K/month and are scoped against specific deliverables and the six ROI metrics (time saved, workflows automated, content output, leads generated, cost reduced, revenue influenced). You can scale up, scale down, or end an engagement month to month.

We'd rather you stay because it's working than because you signed a 12-month paper.

Principles

A short list we actually follow.

  • Ship in 30 days, not 360. If it takes longer than a month to see real output, we scoped wrong.
  • Measure everything that matters. If it doesn't show up in your P&L or your calendar, we don't claim it.
  • Keep humans in the loop. Voice, judgment, relationships — those stay with your team.
  • Be honest about fit. If H&R isn't right for you, we'll say so and usually know who is.
  • Own the stack. You keep every prompt, template, workflow, and dashboard we build. It's yours.
